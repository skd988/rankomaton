      About

      Welcome to Rankomaton!

      Whether you want to rank songs, movies, books or your group of friends (weirdo!), this is the place for you!

      Simply write your list of whatevers into the box on the right and click "rank!". You will be presented with two elements from your list at a time, and be asked to compare them. Choose the "better" one according to whatever standard you want, and move to the next comparison (you can also use the up and down arrow keys if you're using a keyboard).

      Use the "Back" button to go back if you regret your last comparison, and the "Stop" button to stop the current rank.

      To have an estimate of how many comparisons will be required, check the info box.

      Once you finished comparing you will be presented with the list of whatevers ranked from best to worst. You can copy it to show your friends!

      To share your list with others, you can use the "share" button, which will copy a link already loaded with the list you made.

      You can also use it to save the progress you've made so far, and return to your ranking later (Note: the "share" button saves the current progress, so if you want to share just the list without the ranking you did so far, stop the rank first).

      Happy Ranking!
